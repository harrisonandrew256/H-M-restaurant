ABOUT H&M Restaurant

H&M Restaurant is a restaurant created for lovers of fine cuisine, sophisticated style and impeccable service. Each stands as a gateway to a memorable dining experience – whether it be an immersion into traditional Ugandan delicacies or an exploration of International gourmet delights. H&M restaurant are inspired by their own story, which will be revealed through creatively presented dishes, signature cocktails and a style befitting a true 5-star experience.
